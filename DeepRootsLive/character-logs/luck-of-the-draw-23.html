Luck of the Draw 23

Feywild journal
Property of Luck
Day: Roughly 309 years (no wonder my back aches I'm 335 years old)

Ive done some more traveling with this group, and I have only become further convinced I would like to continue traveling with this group as I work on rebuilding my reputation and legacy, not that I had much of either before. Our journey has taken us to the Scarlet City, finally beyond the Krumholtz Mountains and very near the Autumn border. To sort of catch us up on entries Ive missed, Corvaux had caught a disease of some kind interacting with a portal to the Abyss when they were still on the material, we have plans on journeying to Silver Maple and dealing with the pale blight, we went aboard the Crimson Gaze to party, fight, drink, and get our fortunes read, I ended up learning exactly how long Ive been away by talking with the crew. Beyond that, mostly shopping for silver, pale blight protection, and my brand new companion Ralph an absolutely beautiful moorbounder that doesn't like to stay still or move from my side.

The previous night, after our party on the Crimson Gaze, I had gotten my first talk with Crowley in quite some time. I haven't just yet explained Crowley to the party, I can't really think how to word that I have a red eyed crowlock visitor in my dreams that gives me information. Not without sounding a little crazy, though it's potentially nowhere near as alarming to this particular group as it would be for anyone else. Crowley in their typical slightly disjointed fashion informed me it was still very necessary to head to Silver Maple, two demon presences were still around, they also mentioned something about siblings that I assumed was not being literal and just referencing that others like myself were sent. Crowley also made it clear to bring silver, holy water, and plenty of food which we were able to find in spades while shopping in the Scarlet City. Well except the food, I may just gather or hunt it myself.

While shopping I met Josiah, a merchant within the city, who had on offer a periapt that could significantly protect Ralph from the Pale Blight. It was very expensive and after all the shopping I was all but broke, so instead I offered up ranger services and have received a list of items to collect for a discount on a future item purchase. Hopefully I can gather the gold and items to be able to afford that periapt for Ralph, his safety has quickly rose to the top of my priorities. To that end, Mirri has made a deal with me for the shards and powder of Flail Snail eggs she had, and I ventured north with Ralph and Corvaux to find some other items. We ended up discovering a cave leading to a Frost Salamander colony where using some rabbits I caught, a nearby tree, and rope, I had set up a simple snare trap to try and get some of the frost salamanders teeth without too taxing of a fight.
Im writing this section while we wait for a salamander to get stuck in the trap, will update after the fight.

Update: It immediately went wrong. First of all it was MASSIVE I knew some surface level knowledge but I certainly wasn't expecting an ice breathing lizard the size of two or three moorbounders put together. Ralph had to pull out of the fight almost immediately, I spent a considerable amount of time just trying to keep them standing and taking mostly missed shots on the salamander with my bow. Corvaux thankfully is an extremely capable fighter, they handled the fight almost single-handedly. In a moment of shock and terror Ralph went down from the salamanders frost breath but I was able to heal them with my slumbering dragon vessel. Basically the only reason we survived is because my trap managed to hold the entire time, though Corvaux might've somehow won regardless given how much damage they dealt.

In the end, we got out of it with three of the salamanders teeth, Corvaux didn't really get anything out of this fight though so I should figure out some way to repay them for the help, it wouldn't have been possible without them after all. I've also decided Ralph simply won't be aiding in future combats, I can train them as a hunting/scouting companion instead of a battle one so they never get that close to death again. Frankly I'm a bit ashamed of how little help I was on the martial front of that fight, I need to be so much stronger than I currently am. We're resting for the night in the city and I believe we're headed to Autumn next. Very excited to finally be out of Winter after so long.

Note to self: Absolutely NEVER sell that slumbering dragon vessel, Ralph would have died without it.
