Description

Early Life

Corvaux was found in a dark closet in Yewdon. Her mother was an Eladrin Druid.
After Corvaux proved her mettle by brokering talks between the factions within Yewdon (her own Druidic compound, an unsavory guild of mages, and a cult of devil worshippers), as well as her ability to traverse the dark forest, she acted as a courier between the three outposts.

Significant Events

Ten days before the campaign began, Corvaux was pulled out of Yewdon by an unknown force. She remembers being assigned a job, trancing, and waking up in a crater in the fields near Tornwing Haven, where Aeren saw her fall.

Peculiarities

Spoiler

Shadar Kai from Yewdon experience "Shadow corruption" in which, throughout their lives, a black birthmark develops on their fingertips, slowly expanding until the entire body is covered.

Corvaux is something of a dark spot on magical radars; divination magic doesn't work on her

Past life Corvaux was a servant to Faen and saw some of the great events of the demon incursion in Silver Maple. She witnessed some spells that were effective against Pale Blight. Corvaux's soul was brought by Dounuma to be reborn in Yewdon, to hopefully eventually regain the memories of the spells needed to combat the Pale Blight in the present. She died in Yewdon before she was able to recover the memories. Dounuma, at the behest of Faen and Lexiphon, revived Corvaux and dropped her near Tornwing Haven.

Current Goals

Wants to find a way to fix her home, possibly by learning about Silver Maple
